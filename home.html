Mystifier - A Competitive Event Based on Sudoku

Feb 16,2024



Centre for Professional Enhancement is going to conduct “Mystifier - A competitive event based on SUDOKU”. This competitive event will introduce students to Sudoku-solving techniques, followed by a timed puzzle challenge. The emphasis will be on informing participants about the advantages of engaging with Sudoku. Focus of the event will be to enhance quick-thinking abilities, boost memory recall, and sharpen concentration of the participants. This event is designed to elevate logical thinking, reduce anxiety, and alleviate stress during puzzle-solving endeavors.




The main objectives of the event are:



  • To assist students in grasping the patterns involved in completing Sudoku grids.



  • To enhance concentration skills for better performance in daily tasks.



  • To strengthen students' logical thinking through engaging activities.



Target audience: The students of all Schools of Lovely Professional University will participate in the event.




Date: 22nd February, 2024




Time: 02:00 PM Onwards




Venue: Block 34-413




Resource Person: Mr. Amit Dobhal




Register at Link: http://tinyurl.com/MystifierBasedOnSudoku
